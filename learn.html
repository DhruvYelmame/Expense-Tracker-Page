Learn How to Use Expense Tracker

Features of Expense Tracker

  • *Add Expense*: Enter the name of the expense and its amount, then click "Add Expense" to save it.
  • *View History*: See a detailed list of all your expenses in the table below the form.
  • *Learn*: learn about the how to Use.
  • *Track Total Spending*: View the total amount spent, updated automatically as you add or delete expenses.
  • *Contact*: Contact our team for any query & suggestion.

How to Use

Follow these steps to make the most of the Expense Tracker:

  1. Go to the *Expense Tracker* page.
  2. Enter the name of an expense (e.g., "Groceries").
  3. Enter the expense amount (e.g., "500").
  4. Click "Add Expense" to save it to the list.
  5. Check the table below for a list of all expenses.

The Expense Tracker is simple yet powerful, designed to help you stay in control of your finances with ease.
